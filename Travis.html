Top Reasons to Take Home a Pup from an Animal Shelter This Year

  • You'll have a work-from-home partner or someone to eagerly greet you when you get home.

  • Adult dogs often come potty-trained and even know some commands like "sit" and stay."

  • Adoption fees are way less than what you would pay to a breeder. Plus your fees help keep the shelter in the business of helping other animals!

  • A dog keeps your mind and body active.

  • You will receive unconditional love.

  • You'll fall compeltely in love and convince others to rescue as well. Multiple lives saved!
